MAPEH Month

Folk Dance

I really enjoyed the performance of every group, especially the Grade 7 and Grade 9. Their energy brought life to the LPSci campus and they showcased their talents while embracing different parts of being a Filipino at the same time.

Street Dance

I did not get to attend much practices for street dance because I was also preparing myself to compete in PedXing. That’s why in the actual performance I felt really restricted because I didn’t fully master the dance, but I had fun and became closer with my batchmates in Fidelity.

Noli Me Tangere

Ibarra: Himig ng Himagsikan

It was my first time acting in a theatre play that’s why I was so stoked from the start. But, again, since I had a competition in PedXing coming up, there were times I had to balance training with rehearsals and there were times I couldn’t attend practices with my other classmates so I felt a bit behind but I eventually got to catch up.


It was really surreal being the one on the stage (in this case, the audi) instead of being the one in the audience, watching. I really appreciated the work and the collective effort every person in Fairness put for our Noli Me Tangere play and it is a core memory of ny highschool life that will for sure echo in my mind for many more years to come.
